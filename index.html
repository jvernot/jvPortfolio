And I couldn't be happier

I think that this one picture pretty perfectly encapsulates me. I am always looking for the next adventure to go on or a new challenege to conquer. Those challenges range from travling new places, or trying all sorts of new foods, to DIY home projects, to trying to shoot my lowest round of golf. Despite any adverse conditions that I might have to deal with, I am always ready to adapt and overcome. However, right now my next big challenge isn't any of the things I mentioned earlier, it's learning to code.

I am a young developer learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, NodeJS and everything in between. Learning to code has been one of the biggest chanllenges I have taken on to date. It has taken more time, dedication, and patience than I could have imagined, but I am loving it. Coding combines many of the different asepcts that I have enjoyed in other previous jobs. It requires independence and motivation, but still fosters collaboration of a team to complete projects. There are best practices, but there is never one right answer, allowing for creativity and individuality.

I still have a ton more to learn, and I am sure many more obstacles to overcome, in my journey to becomeing a developer. But I am ready for all of the hurdles and I am excited by how far I've already come to this point. Chcek out some of my completed projects below.
